What is NR Console?

NR-Console is a stand-alone Java program that runs on Windows, Linux and Mac OSX. NR-Console is designed to help you get started working with the DyIO and other Bowler devices by giving you quick access to all of DyIO's available features before you are ready to begin programming in Java. Included on this page are directions for setting up NR Console, using NR-Console to connect to a DyIO, and descriptions of the components of NR Console and how to use them.

Launching NR Console

The first thing you will see when you launch the NR-Console program is a window prompting you to connect to your DyIO. At this point, you have a choice of how to connect to your DyIO. By default, the "Serial" tab is selected on the connection window. You should use this tab to connect to a DyIO that is plugged into your computer via USB, or direct serial.
